Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. Information We Collect

WeLookup does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

The extension does not receive or process:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • IP addresses
  • Cookies
  • Account data
  • Browsing history
  • Form data
  • Payment information
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII)

We have no servers receiving data from the extension.

2. How the Extension Works

WeLookup analyzes public webpage resources such as:

  • Publicly loaded scripts
  • Network resource hostnames
  • Browser performance metrics (e.g., TTFB, FCP, Requests)

This analysis:

  • Happens locally on your device
  • Does not identify or track you
  • Does not send any data to WeLookup servers

The extension does not inject ads, modify pages, or interact with websites beyond reading publicly available data.

3. Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome’s session storage only to keep temporary per-tab scan results.

  • Data never leaves your device
  • Data clears when the tab ends
  • Nothing is synced or shared

4. No Tracking, No Analytics

WeLookup does not use cookies, analytics, telemetry, fingerprinting, or tracking of any kind.

5. Permissions Explanation

  • “tabs” — used to identify the active tab so scan results can be displayed.
  • “storage.session” — stores audit results locally per tab.
  • “<all_urls>” — required to read public webpage resources for tool detection.

None of these permissions collect personal data.

6. Third-Party Data

WeLookup does not send data to any third parties. No outside service receives or accesses your data.

7. Children’s Privacy

WeLookup does not collect data from users of any age, including children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the extension evolves. Updates will be posted on welookup.info.

support@welookup.info
