Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. Information We Collect

WeLookup does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

The extension does not receive or process:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • IP addresses
  • Cookies
  • Account data
  • Browsing history
  • Form data
  • Payment information
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII)

We have no servers receiving data from the extension.

2. How the Extension Works

WeLookup analyzes public webpage resources such as:

  • Publicly loaded scripts
  • Network resource hostnames
  • Browser performance metrics (e.g., TTFB, FCP, Requests)

This analysis:

  • Happens locally on your device
  • Does not identify or track you
  • Does not send any data to WeLookup servers

The extension does not modify pages, inject ads, store logs, or interact with websites beyond reading publicly available data.

3. Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome’s session storage only to temporarily keep the latest scan result within your browser tab.

  • Data never leaves your device
  • Data is cleared when the tab/session ends
  • You can delete it anytime by closing or reloading the page

4. No Tracking, No Analytics

WeLookup does not use cookies, analytics, telemetry, fingerprinting, or any form of user tracking.

5. Permissions Explanation

  • "tabs" — used only to detect the active tab for displaying results.
  • "storage.session" — used only to save scan results locally per tab.
  • "<all_urls>" — required to scan publicly visible webpage resources.

None of these permissions collect personal data.

6. Third-Party Data

WeLookup does not send any data to third-party services. No external service can access your data through this extension.

7. Children’s Privacy

WeLookup collects no data from users of any age, including children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the extension evolves. Changes will be posted on welookup.info.

9. Contact

If you have questions about privacy or the operation of the extension, contact:

support@welookup.info
