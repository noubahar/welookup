Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. Information We Collect

WeLookup does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

The extension does not receive or process:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • IP addresses
  • Cookies
  • Account data
  • Browsing history
  • Form data
  • Payment information
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII)

We have no servers receiving data from the extension.

2. How the Extension Works

WeLookup analyzes publicly available webpage resources including:

  • Loaded scripts
  • Network request hostnames
  • Browser performance metrics (such as TTFB, FCP, Request count)

This analysis:

  • Runs entirely on your device
  • Does not identify or track you
  • Does not send any data to WeLookup servers

3. Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome session storage only to temporarily store scan results inside your current browser tab.

  • Data never leaves your device
  • Data clears automatically when the session ends
  • You may clear it anytime by closing or refreshing the tab

4. No Tracking, No Analytics

WeLookup does not use analytics, cookies, telemetry, fingerprinting, or any form of user tracking.

5. Permissions

  • "tabs" — used only to determine the currently active tab.
  • "storage.session" — used only to save scan results locally.
  • "<all_urls>" — required for reading publicly visible resources on the page.

The extension does not use permissions to collect personal data.

6. Third-Party Data

WeLookup does not send any data to third-party services. No external service can access your data through this extension.

7. Children’s Privacy

The extension collects no data from any users, including children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this Privacy Policy will be posted on welookup.info.
